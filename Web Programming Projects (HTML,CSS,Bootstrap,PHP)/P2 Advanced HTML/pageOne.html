Self-portrait in a Straw Hat By: Louise Elisabeth Lebrun
The Parinting appears, after cleaning, to be an autograph replica of a picture, the original of which was painted in Brussels in 1782 in free imitation of Ruben's 'Chapeau de Pallie', which LeBrun had seen in Antwerp. It was exhibited in Paris in 1782 at the Salon de la Correspondance. LeBrun's original is recoded in a private collection in France. $700
